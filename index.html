Our Mission

We Bring the Lab to the Classroom

Project Uddipon (উদ্দীপন — "inspiration" or "ignition") is a nationwide STEM empowerment initiative by ZAN Tech. We design and deliver free, high-quality workshops on Robotics, Programming, AI/ML and IoT for students across Bangladesh.

"Ensuring that no talented Bangladeshi student is left behind in the Fourth Industrial Revolution — regardless of geography, gender or income."
  • 100% free for all participating students and institutions
  • Delivered by practicing engineers from ZAN Tech
  • All robotics kits, materials and equipment brought by our team
  • Focus on rural, semi-urban and underserved communities
  • Post-workshop STEM club setup and ongoing mentorship

Programs

What We Deliver in Every Workshop

Each session is hands-on, project-based, and tailored to the students' age and level. No lectures — just building, coding, and discovering.

Robotics Workshops
Students build line-following and obstacle-avoiding robots using Arduino, sensors and motors. Fully hands-on — every student builds their own circuit.
Arduino Sensors Motors Class 6 – University
Programming & AI Basics
Beginner-friendly introduction to programming logic and AI/ML concepts through visual, real-life examples — no prior experience required.
Python AI/ML Scratch All Levels
IoT Bootcamps
Practical Internet-of-Things projects: smart monitoring, sensor data collection and connected devices tailored to local problems and student imagination.
IoT ESP32 Smart Devices
Girls in STEM
Dedicated sessions at girls' schools — designed to build confidence, inspire curiosity, and break gender barriers in technology education across Bangladesh.
Girls' Schools Empowerment Role Models
STEM Club Formation
After the workshop, we help institutions launch Robotics or STEM clubs — with starter project ideas, resources and long-term mentorship from ZAN Tech.
Clubs Mentorship Long-term
Teacher Training
We train teachers on robotics basics and modern STEM teaching methods so hands-on learning continues well after our team leaves the campus.
Capacity Building Curriculum Faculty

Process

From First Contact to Workshop Day

Inviting Project Uddipon is simple. From your first message to the final workshop — a smooth, five-step process handled almost entirely by our team.

Start the Process Now
1

You Reach Out

Send us a WhatsApp, email or Facebook message with your institution name, location, student count and preferred dates.

2

We Plan Together

Our team contacts you within 48 hours to confirm topics, session format, date and on-site logistics — completely customised to your students.

3

We Arrive Fully Equipped

ZAN Tech engineers visit your campus with all robotics kits, computers, materials and activities — zero cost to your institution.

4

Students Build & Learn

A 3–6 hour hands-on session. Every student builds, codes and experiments. We handle everything; you watch the transformation.

5

Ongoing Support & Club Setup

We help launch a STEM club and provide online resources, project guides and mentorship so the learning never stops.
